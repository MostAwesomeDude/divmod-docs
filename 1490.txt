== Divmod Sine ==

A voice over IP application server.


Sine provides:
 * SIP Registrar
 * SIP Proxy
 * Third party call control (3PCC)
 * Voice-mail
 * Through-the-web configuration
 * [wiki:DivmodMantissa Divmod Mantissa] integration
